Hello! I'm Federico, a Project Manager & Full Stack Developer. My leadership journey started in 2006 and spans over 14 years: from managing a 9-person team in hospitality, to administering a hotel, to coordinating airmen at the Italian Senate's Honor Company in Rome, up to leading a 12-person welding department delivering the missile compartment of the Italian Navy's latest frigate for Leonardo S.p.A., along with components for submarines and Fincantieri cruise ships.
